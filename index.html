About Me

Hello there! I'm a seasoned software tester calling Staffordshire home, where I navigate life's adventures alongside my wife and two young daughters.

My journey in testing kicked off in 2012, and since then, I've accumulated over a decade of valuable experience. I take pride in my commitment to software testing and have witnessed the industry's fascinating evolution.

Before I found my footing in testing, I pursued a degree in History at Lancaster University. Post-university, I explored roles in a contact center, seeking direction without a clear plan.

Throughout my testing tenure, I've explored diverse business sectors, from general retail to pharmaceuticals and automotive. My expertise spans a range of systems, from straightforward web applications to intricate Microsoft ERP solutions.

Beyond professional achievements, I crossed paths with my wife, Leanne, in the early stages of my testing career. She, too, brings a wealth of experience to the software testing arena.

Embracing recent family-focused company policies, I find comfort in working from home, striking a balance between professional commitments and family life. In 2021, recognizing the need for a dedicated workspace, I transformed our garage into a personal office space, fostering productivity.

In my downtime, you'll often find me engrossed in audiobooks, exercising, or watching NBA games.

As I reflect on my journey, I find fulfillment in the convergence of family, professional growth, and a genuine passion for the intricate world of software testing.
